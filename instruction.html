How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

The game is played on a 3x3 grid.

Player X goes first, then Player O. Players take turns placing their mark in an empty cell.

The first player to get 3 marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.

If all cells are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game ends in a draw.
